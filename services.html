Pre-Purchase Inspection

Buying an RV is a major investment. Private sellers and even dealers may not disclose every issue. A professional pre-purchase inspection by Boomer gives you an unbiased, expert assessment of everything from the chassis to the roof before you sign anything.

Chassis & Frame — Rust, cracks, weld failures, and structural integrity assessment
Mechanical Systems — Engine, transmission, brakes, and suspension evaluation
Electrical Systems — 12V, 120V, generator, shore power, and battery condition
Roof & Seals — Delamination, leaks, caulk failure, and roof membrane condition
Appliances & Systems — Water heater, refrigerator, furnace, AC, and slideout operation

We come to the RV. Inspection can be done at the seller's location — no need to move the unit before you own it.
