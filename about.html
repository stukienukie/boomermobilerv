The Story

A Career Built on the Road

Mike "Boomer" Hennessy didn't learn mechanics in a classroom. He built his expertise over four decades across some of the most demanding environments in the world — from the Australian Outback to international racing circuits to the high-stakes world of national fleet management.

His career began with a deep immersion in powersport mechanics, eventually rising to the position of National Technical & Warranty Manager for Triumph Motorcycles in Australia — one of the most demanding technical oversight roles in the industry. Managing warranty claims and technical standards across a national dealer network demands a level of diagnostic precision that most mechanics never develop.

From there, Boomer moved into heavy fleet operations, working with Polaris and Victory Motorcycles, developing expertise in large-scale mechanical operations, parts logistics, and field service under conditions where failure isn't an option.

His background in off-road racing and Australian Outback operations gave him something that classroom training never could: the ability to diagnose and repair complex mechanical systems with limited tools, in the field, under pressure, when the stakes are real.

Now based in Midvale, Utah, Mike brings every bit of that experience to RV owners across the Salt Lake Valley — as the sole technician, the person who answers the phone, does the work, and stands behind it.

Field Story

The Infected Rain Rescue

International touring band Infected Rain was mid-tour when their equipment trailer's leaf spring assemblies failed at a Utah venue. The failure meant their gear couldn't move — and neither could the tour.

Boomer arrived on-site, assessed the damage, and executed a same-day structural repair: removing the failed 4-leaf spring assemblies and replacing them with upgraded 5-leaf configurations. The trailer left under its own load the same evening.

This is what 40+ years of real-world mechanical experience looks like in practice — not a repair manual, but a deep instinct for what a system needs and how to deliver it under pressure.
